Title:
Photograph: Tiny Broadwick lands in Griffith Park, Las Angeles Ca.
Date Created:
1913-06-21
Description:
A picture of the first landing Tiny Broadwick made from a airplane, at Griffith Park, June 21, 1913. "This picture was taken in action shot, and was a stand up landing." Tiny was born in Granville County, North Carolina and she was the first woman to make a parachute jump from an airplane.
